Memo Master Lite

MemoMaster Lite: It automatically converts multilingual and multi-speaker meeting audio into structured Minutes of Meeting (MoM). Using Whisper + AI models, it extracts summaries, key discussion points, assigned tasks, deadlines, and speaker-wise notes. The system supports live recording, file upload, timeline extraction, and downloadable PDF generation. Tech Stack: Python, FastAPI, Whisper, React, Streamlit
